
## COLOR PALETTE

**Primary:**
- Deep Teal #1A3A3A — headers, primary buttons, key UI
- Graphite #2D2D2D — body text, grounding elements
- Slate Teal #2E5555 — hover states, depth layers

**Neutrals:**
- Off-White #FAFAF8 — main canvas, hero backgrounds
- Fog Grey #F2F2EF — card backgrounds, section dividers
- Mist White #E8E8E4 — input fields, layered surfaces
- Soft Stone #D4D4CF — dividers, thin borders

**Accents:**
- Soft Amber #E8A86B — CTAs, progress indicators, highlights
- Pale Coral #F4C9A3 — badges, soft highlights
- Sunrise Gold #D4955A — premium features, energy peaks

**Semantic:**
- Sage Green #6B9B7A — success, completion, recovery
- Muted Ochre #C4A35A — caution, energy dips
- Twilight Blue #5A7A8C — sleep data, calm zones

**Gradients:**
- Hero Fade: #1A3A3A → #2E5555 → #FAFAF8
- Card Glow: #FAFAF8 → #F2F2EF
- Progress Arc: #E8A86B → #D4955A
- Calm Overlay: rgba(26,58,58,0.85) → transparent

**Typography Colors:**
- Headlines: Graphite #2D2D2D
- Body: Graphite @ 85% #3D3D3D
- Captions: Graphite @ 60% #6B6B6B
- On Dark: Off-White #FAFAF8
- Links: Deep Teal #1A3A3A
- Accent: Soft Amber #E8A86B

## VISUAL IDENTITY

**Style:** Minimal, calm, premium, high whitespace, thin-line icons, geometric shapes, soft gradients (fog grey, mist white, teal → graphite fade), soft amber/coral pops for highlights. Human silhouettes used sparingly — always calm, natural.

**Inspirations:** Apple product marketing, WHOOP performance visuals, Strava data-share tiles, Notion minimalism, scientific calmness (biometrics, rhythms, waveforms), wellness as identity/ritual.

**Typography:** Geometric sans-serif, thin-weight, clean hierarchy
